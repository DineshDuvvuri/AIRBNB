    Introduce

    Railways have played a pivotal role in shaping the way people travel and connect across vast distances. Since their inception, railways have revolutionized transportation, offering a reliable and efficient means of getting from one place to another.The development of railways can be traced back to the early 19th century, with the advent of steam-powered locomotives, which allowed for the rapid expansion of rail networks across continents.

    History

    The concept of rail transportation dates back to ancient times, with examples such as the Diolkos in ancient Greece, which was a paved trackway used for transporting ships overland. However, it wasn't until the 19th century that railways as we know them today began to take shape.One of the most significant achievements in railway history was the construction of transcontinental railways, which linked the east and west coasts of vast continents such as North America and Australia

    More Details

    Medha Servo Drives Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian company specializing in designing and manufacturing control and automation products for the railway industry. Founded in 1984 by Mr. Y. Gopala Krishna, Medha has grown to become a leading supplier of propulsion, control, and communication systems for railways. The company is headquartered in Hyderabad, India, and operates manufacturing facilities and research and development centers across the country.
